Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our construction services.

Project timelines vary based on size and complexity. A standard residential home typically takes 6-12 months, while commercial projects can range from 12-36 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline specific to your project.
Our pricing includes all labor, materials, project management, permits, and standard finishes. Customizations, premium materials, and site-specific challenges may incur additional costs, which we'll identify during the planning phase.
We build contingency time into all schedules and maintain strong relationships with suppliers to minimize delays. If delays occur, we communicate immediately, explain the impact, and present solutions to get back on track.
We provide a 2-year workmanship warranty on all projects. Many materials come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 10-25 years. Specific warranty details will be outlined in your contract.
